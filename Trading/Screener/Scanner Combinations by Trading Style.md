### **I. Top Ranked Scanner Combinations by Trading Style**

Here is a ranked list of powerful screener combinations, mixing technical and fundamental filters depending on the specific trading or investing case.

#### **Rank 1: The Trend-Following Swing Trader (Best Overall)**

- **Best For:** Catching established, strong uptrends for short to medium-term holds (days to weeks).
    
- **Risk Level:** Moderate.
    
- **The Combination (Technical Focus):**
    
    - **Trend:** `Price > SMA 50` AND `Price > SMA 200` (Ensures the stock is in a long-term uptrend).
        
    - **Momentum:** `MACD (26,12,9)` -> Bullish Crossover (MACD line crosses above signal line).
        
    - **Confirmation:** `Volume > VMA (20)` (Ensures the upward move is backed by higher-than-average volume).
        
    - **Avoid Overbought:** `RSI (14)` -> Between 40 and 65 (Ensures you aren't buying at the very peak).
        

#### **Rank 2: The "Smart Money" Tracker (Best for DSE)**

- **Best For:** Finding stocks where directors or institutions are quietly accumulating shares. Highly effective in the Bangladesh market.
    
- **Risk Level:** Low to Moderate.
    
- **The Combination (Fundamental + Technical):**
    
    - **Insider Buying:** `Director Holding % Change` -> Positive (Increasing).
        
    - **Institutional Backing:** `Institute Holding % Change` -> Positive (Increasing).
        
    - **Company Health:** `Last Year EPS Growth` -> Positive (> 10%).
        
    - **Entry Trigger:** `Price > SMA 20` (Wait for short-term price momentum to align with the smart money).
        

#### **Rank 3: The Undervalued Growth Finder (CANSLIM Style)**

- **Best For:** Medium to long-term investing. Finding fundamentally strong companies that are ready to break out.
    
- **Risk Level:** Low.
    
- **The Combination (Heavy Fundamental + Light Technical):**
    
    - **Value:** `PE Ratio` -> Between 8 and 15 (Avoids severely overvalued stocks).
        
    - **Consistent Growth:** `5 Years EPS Growth` -> Positive (> 10-15%).
        
    - **Recent Growth:** `Quarterly EPS growth` -> Positive.
        
    - **Technical Support:** `Price > SMA 200` (Never buy a "cheap" stock if it is in a long-term death spiral).
        

#### **Rank 4: The Breakout / Momentum Day Trader**

- **Best For:** Intraday or 1-2 day holds. High-speed trading based on sudden market interest.
    
- **Risk Level:** High.
    
- **The Combination (Pure Technical):**
    
    - **Volatility/Speed:** `Price Change` -> > 2% to 5% today.
        
    - **Massive Liquidity:** `Volume` -> At least 2x or 3x greater than `VMA (20)`.
        
    - **Strength:** `RSI (14)` -> > 60 (Indicates strong upward momentum).
        
    - **Trend Acceleration:** `Price > EMA 20` (Using Exponential Moving Average because it reacts faster to price changes than SMA).
        

#### **Rank 5: The Oversold Reversal / Bottom Fishing**

- **Best For:** Contrarian traders looking to buy panic selling and catch the "bounce."
    
- **Risk Level:** High (Requires strict stop losses).
    
- **The Combination (Technical Mean-Reversion):**
    
    - **Extreme Fear:** `RSI (14)` -> < 30 (Highly oversold).
        
    - **Price Extremes:** `BB (20,2)` (Bollinger Bands) -> Price is touching or falling below the lower band.
        
    - **Capitulation Volume:** Volume is significantly higher than usual (suggests the last of the weak hands are selling).
        
    - _Tip:_ Wait for a bullish `Candlestick` pattern (like a hammer or engulfing candle) before entering.
        

#### **Rank 6: The Safe Dividend Investor**

- **Best For:** Retirees or passive income seekers wanting low-volatility portfolio anchors.
    
- **Risk Level:** Very Low.
    
- **The Combination (Pure Fundamental):**
    
    - **Yield History:** `Last Year Cash Declaration` -> Look for high dividend yields (e.g., > 5%).
        
    - **Safety:** `Category` -> 'A' Category stocks only.
        
    - **Profitability:** `Last NPAT Growth` (Net Profit After Tax) -> Positive.
        
    - **Discount Entry:** `RSI (14)` -> < 40 (Buy these strong dividend stocks when the market dips).
